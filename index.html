Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

Vanshika's – Taste of Tradition is a boutique sweet & confectionery house nestled in the heart of Pune. We believe that every bite should tell a story — of heritage, care, and the finest natural ingredients.

Our journey began with a simple passion: reviving authentic Indian sweets made the way they were meant to be made. Each modak, each sweet, is prepared by hand with meticulous attention to hygiene, flavour, and presentation.

Whether you're celebrating a festival, gifting someone special, or simply treating yourself, we are here to make every moment sweeter.
