About Our Restaurant

Established in 2010, Golden Dragon has been serving the community with authentic Asian cuisine made from the freshest ingredients. Our chefs bring decades of experience from across Asia to create dishes that will transport your taste buds.

From traditional dim sum to spicy Szechuan specialties, we offer a menu that satisfies every palate. Join us for dine-in, takeout, or catering services.
